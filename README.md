## 🎯 Strategic Business Insights
- **Workforce Maturity:** Identified an average working tenure of **11.28 years**, indicating a highly experienced workforce.
- **Educational Pipeline:** 60%+ of employees originate from Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds, highlighting specialized talent concentration.
- **Departmental Density:** 'Research Scientist' and 'Sales Executive' roles were identified as the primary organizational pillars.
- **Operational Metrics:** Real-time tracking of Hourly Rates ($65.89 avg) and Overtime trends (28.3% Yes).

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
- **Data Engineering:** End-to-end ETL process using **Power Query** to clean and structure the HR Dataset.
- **Advanced DAX:** Developed complex measures for tenure calculation, income averages, and dynamic filtering.
- **UI/UX Excellence:** Created a dual-theme system (Green/Blue) to cater to different reporting environments.
- **Interactivity:** Full cross-filtering capability across all 6 visualizations for granular insights.
